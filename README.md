## 🌡️ About This Project

This project will focus on analyzing **summer weather conditions** and understanding how they impact:

- 🌍 Human life & daily activities  
- ⚠️ Health and safety risks  
- 💼 Business opportunities  
- 📈 Market trends and product demand  

---

## 🎯 What I Plan To Do

✔️ Study weather patterns during the summer season  
✔️ Identify harmful effects like heatwaves and dehydration  
✔️ Suggest practical safety and precautionary measures  
✔️ Analyze which products and services become popular  
✔️ Provide business recommendations based on seasonal demand  

---

## 🌦️ Planned Features

### 🔍 Weather Analysis
- Study temperature trends  
- Observe extreme weather conditions  
- Analyze impact on lifestyle  

### ⚠️ Impact Study
- Health risks (heatstroke, dehydration)  
- Social and economic effects  
- Energy and water consumption  

### 🛡️ Safety Guide
- Personal precautions  
- Household safety tips  
- Awareness recommendations
