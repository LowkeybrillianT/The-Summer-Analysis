# ⚠️ Day 3 – Problem Identification

## 📍 Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

---

## 🌡️ Weather-Related Problems

- Extreme heat during afternoon hours  
- Lack of shade in busy areas  
- Increased discomfort due to urban structures  

---

## 👤 Health Problems

- Dehydration 💧  
- Heat exhaustion 😓  
- Headaches and fatigue 🧠  

---

## 🏠 Daily Life Problems

- Reduced outdoor productivity  
- Difficulty in commuting during peak heat  
- Increased dependence on cooling appliances  

---

## ⚡ Resource Problems

- Higher electricity consumption  
- Increased water usage  
- Pressure on local infrastructure  

---

## 💡 Core Problem Insight

Summer heat creates multiple interconnected problems affecting health, daily life, and resources. Urban areas like Nagawara experience stronger impact due to traffic and concrete surroundings.
