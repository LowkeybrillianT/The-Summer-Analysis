# 🌞 Day 1 – Weather Observation

## 📍 Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

## 🌍 Area Description
Nagawara is a busy urban area with residential buildings, traffic, and commercial activity. Due to concrete structures and vehicles, heat feels more intense during summer.

## 🌡️ Temperature (Approx)
Morning: Moderate (25–28°C)  
Afternoon: Very hot (34–37°C)  
Evening: Warm  

## 🌤️ Weather Condition
Mostly sunny and hot

## 👤 Personal Observation
- Afternoon heat felt very strong  
- Sweating increased while going outside  
- Felt tired during peak hours  

## ⚠️ Impact Noticed
- Low energy in afternoon  
- Needed more water  
- Avoided going out in peak heat  

## 🧠 Initial Thought
Urban areas like Nagawara experience stronger heat impact due to buildings and traffic, affecting daily comfort and productivity.
