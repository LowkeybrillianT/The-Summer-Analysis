# ⚠️ Day 2 – Impact Analysis (Summer Heat)

## 📍 Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

---

## 🌡️ Health Impact

- Increased sweating and dehydration 💧  
- Risk of heat exhaustion and fatigue 😓  
- Headaches and low energy levels 🧠  

---

## 👤 Daily Life Impact

- Reduced outdoor activities during afternoon  
- Increased dependency on fans, coolers, and AC ❄️  
- More frequent consumption of water and cold drinks 🧃  

---

## ⚡ Energy & Resource Impact

- Higher electricity usage due to cooling appliances  
- Increased water consumption  
- Stress on local resources  

---

## 💡 Insight

Summer heat significantly affects both physical health and daily lifestyle. It forces people to change routines, reduce outdoor exposure, and rely more on cooling and hydration solutions.
