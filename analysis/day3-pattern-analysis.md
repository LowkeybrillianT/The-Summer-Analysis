# 🔍 Day 3 – Pattern Analysis

## 📍 Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

---

## 📊 Observed Patterns

### 🌡️ Temperature Pattern
- Heat increases from morning to afternoon  
- Peak temperature during mid-day  
- Slight decrease in evening  

---

### 👥 Human Behavior Pattern
- People avoid going out during peak hours  
- Increased water and cold drink consumption  
- Preference for shaded or indoor environments  

---

### 🛍️ Market Pattern
- Higher sales of cold beverages during afternoon  
- Local vendors experience more demand  
- Quick-consumption products sell faster  

---

## 🔗 Relationship Between Factors

- Rising temperature → Increased discomfort  
- Discomfort → Change in behavior  
- Behavior change → Increase in product demand  

---

## 💡 Insight

There is a direct relationship between temperature rise and consumer behavior. As heat increases, demand for cooling and hydration products also increases significantly.
