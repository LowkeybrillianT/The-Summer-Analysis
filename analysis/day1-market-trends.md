# 📈 Day 1 – Market Trends (Nagawara Area)

## 🛍️ Observation Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

## 🔍 What I Observed

During summer, local shops and street vendors are selling more:

- Cold drinks 🧃  
- Packaged water bottles 💧  
- Ice creams 🍦  
- Fresh juices (lemon, sugarcane)  

## 🔥 Trending Items

- Coconut water  
- Lemon juice  
- Soft drinks  
- Ice cream  

## 👥 Consumer Behavior

- People prefer cold and refreshing items  
- Increased demand during afternoon hours  
- Quick purchase decisions due to heat  

## 💡 Business Insight

Summer heat is increasing demand for hydration and cooling products. Small vendors and local shops benefit more due to accessibility and quick service.
