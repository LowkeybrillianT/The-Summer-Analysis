# 🧠 Day 1 – AI Prediction

## 🤖 Objective
To use Artificial Intelligence to predict summer market trends and compare them with real-world observations.

## 📊 AI Predictions

Based on summer conditions in urban areas like Nagawara, AI suggests:

- High demand for cold beverages 🧃  
- Increased sales of ice creams 🍦  
- Growth in cooling appliances like fans and air coolers ❄️  
- Rising demand for sunscreen and skincare products 🧴  

## 📈 Consumer Behavior Prediction

- People will prefer quick hydration options  
- More outdoor fatigue leading to higher cold drink consumption  
- Shift towards convenience-based purchases  

## 💡 Insight

AI predicts that summer heat significantly influences both consumer behavior and product demand, especially in urban environments.
