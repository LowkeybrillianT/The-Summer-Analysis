# 🛡️ Day 3 – Solutions & Precaution Strategy

## 📍 Location
Nagawara, Bangalore

---

## 👤 Individual Precautions

- Drink plenty of water regularly 💧  
- Avoid going out during peak heat hours ☀️  
- Wear light and breathable clothing 👕  
- Use sunscreen and protective gear 🧴  

---

## 🏠 Household Solutions

- Use curtains to reduce indoor heat  
- Maintain proper ventilation  
- Use fans, coolers, or AC efficiently ❄️  

---

## 🏢 Business Solutions

- Provide shaded areas for customers  
- Offer cooling products and hydration options  
- Adjust business hours to avoid peak heat  

---

## 🌍 Community-Level Solutions

- Increase green cover (plants and trees) 🌱  
- Improve water availability  
- Promote awareness about heat safety  

---

## 💡 Final Insight

Proper precautions and smart solutions can significantly reduce the negative impact of summer heat. Awareness and preparation are key to managing seasonal challenges effectively.
