# 📄 Executive Summary

## 📍 Project: Summer Weather Analysis

This project analyzes the impact of summer weather in Nagawara, Bangalore, focusing on how rising temperatures affect human health, daily life, and market behavior.

The study combines real-world observations, basic data collection, and AI-based predictions to understand seasonal patterns.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Temperature peaks during afternoon hours, causing discomfort and fatigue  
- Increased demand for cooling and hydration products  
- Consumer behavior shifts towards quick and refreshing purchases  
- Urban areas experience stronger heat impact due to infrastructure  

---

## 💼 Business Insight

Seasonal demand creates strong opportunities in:
- Cold beverages  
- Cooling appliances  
- Summer clothing  
- Skincare products  

---

## 🧠 Conclusion (Short)

Summer heat significantly influences both lifestyle and economic activity. Businesses and individuals who adapt to seasonal changes can reduce risks and take advantage of opportunities.
