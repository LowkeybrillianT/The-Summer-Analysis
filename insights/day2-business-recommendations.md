# 💼 Day 2 – Business Recommendations

## 📍 Based on Analysis (Nagawara, Bangalore)

---

## 🛍️ High Demand Products

- Cold beverages (water, juice, soft drinks) 🧃  
- Ice creams and frozen items 🍦  
- Cooling appliances (fans, coolers) ❄️  
- Summer clothing (light cotton wear) 👕  
- Skincare products (sunscreen) 🧴  

---

## 📈 Business Opportunities

### 1. Small Vendors
- Selling fresh juices, coconut water  
- Mobile drink stalls near busy areas  

### 2. Retail Shops
- Stock more cooling and hydration products  
- Offer combo deals (drink + snack)  

### 3. Online Businesses
- Promote summer-related products  
- Quick delivery for cold beverages  

---

## 👥 Consumer Behavior Insight

- Customers prefer quick and refreshing products  
- Demand increases during peak afternoon hours  
- Local and affordable products sell more  

---

## 💡 Final Insight

Summer creates strong business opportunities in hydration, cooling, and comfort-related products. Businesses that adapt quickly to seasonal demand can increase sales significantly.
